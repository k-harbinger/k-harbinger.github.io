This is a heatmap showing vision by region. We can see that Sumeru has a huge amount of Dendro characters at 5, and Mondstadt has a huge amount of Cryo characters at 5 as well. This makes sense for Sumeru, with the patch the Dendro element was released in being the same as the one this region was released.

We also see a darkness for Snezhnaya, owing to the fact that only one Snezhnaya character had been released at the time the data was collected.

Surprises:

I was surprised to see that Mondstadt had so many Cryo characters, given that it's the nation of Anemo.

I was also surprised to see that Dendro characters have lower-than-average DEF. This could be because they are a recent addition, and DEF has become less-and-less relevant to the meta of the game as time has gone on. It could also be because Dendro characters need both ATK and EM, while most characters of other elements don't need EM.
